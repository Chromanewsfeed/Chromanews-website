⚠️ Help & Troubleshooting
Quick fixes for common issues
📰 Articles not loading?
On desktop press Ctrl+Shift+R. On mobile, close the tab completely and reopen chromanews.com.
🕐 Stuck on old news?
Clear your browser cache. On Chrome: Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data.
📄 Summary not working?
Summaries are AI-generated and may take a few seconds. Article may be paywalled.
📈 Markets not updating?
Close the Markets panel and reopen it. Market data refreshes every 5 minutes.
